rolycoly coal pokémon
most of rolycoly body has the same composition as coal . fittingly , this pokémon was first discovered in coal mines about 400 years ago .
rolycoly can race around like a unicycle , even on rough , rocky terrain . burning coal sustains rolycoly .
on sunny days , rolycoly will come out onto grassy plains and roll around . then rolycoly will eat the grass that gets tangled in rolycoly wheel-like leg .
chunks of the surface of this pokémon ’ s body that have grown old and flaked off have long been used for fuel as an alternative to coal .
